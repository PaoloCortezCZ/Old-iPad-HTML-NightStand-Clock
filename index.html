Why NightStand?

⏰ Five clock faces

Flip cards, LED 7-segment, 70's square analog, minimal round, Swiss station. All resizable.

🌙 Night red mode

Auto-switches to dim red on your schedule (22–07 by default) or with one tap on ☾.

🌦 Local weather

Temperature, feels-like, humidity, wind, sunrise/sunset and tomorrow's forecast. Auto-located or any city.

₿ Bitcoin ticker

Live BTC/USD with 24h change and sparkline — green up, red down.

📅 Date & name days

Day, date and the Czech svátek in the bottom bar.

🕯 Made for old iPads

Runs on iPad Mini 4 era Safari. No frameworks, no build, a single HTML file. Keeps the screen awake.

Setup in one minute

  1. Open the clock in Safari on your iPad and allow location.
  2. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen — it becomes a fullscreen app with no browser bars.
  3. Set Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock → Never.
  4. Put the iPad on a stand, charge overnight, sleep well.
Tip: everything is configurable via the dim ⚙︎ button in the bottom-left corner of the clock — clock style, fonts, sizes, night hours, °C/°F, location.
